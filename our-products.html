Our Products

Airavat Industries delivering the pure essence of jaggery for a healthier, more sustainable future. Our products showcase the versatility of jaggery and its wide range of applications in cooking, baking and as a sweet treat.

Jaggery Blocks

Solid chunk-like pieces of jaggery that can be grated or chopped for use in cooking and baking.

Jaggery Powder

Finely ground jaggery convenient for easy incorporation into recipes and beverages.

Jaggery Syrup

A liquid form of jaggery often used as a natural sweetener in drinks and desserts.

Jaggery Candy

Sweet confections made primarily from jaggery sometimes combined with nuts or dried fruits.

Jaggery Cubes

Small cube-shaped pieces of jaggery that are often used for convenience in recipes or as a sweet treat.

Date Palm Jaggery

Made from the sap of date palms this variety has a distinct flavor and is often used in Middle Eastern and Asian cuisines.

Sugarcane Jaggery

The most common type derived from sugarcane juice and used worldwide in a variety of culinary applications.

Jaggery Balls

Small round balls of jaggery sometimes mixed with spices or grains used as a snack or energy bite.

Jaggery Paste

A smooth spreadable form of jaggery used as a sweetener in sauces or as a filling in confectioneries.

Jaggery Tea

Tea blends or powders that incorporate jaggery as a sweetener offering a unique flavor compared to traditional sugar.
